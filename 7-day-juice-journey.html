Fasting Fun Fact

Fasting has been used for its health benefits through out time. For centuries different religions and civilisations have practiced fasting claiming it brings physical and spiritual renewal and revival, Pythagoras was among many who extolled the virtues of fasting. In primitive cultures a fast was often demanded before going to war or as part of a coming-of-age ritual. It was used to soothe an angry deity and by native North Americans as a rite to avoid catastrophes such as famine.

Judaism has several annual fasting days eg Yom Kippur, Muslims fast during the holy month of Ramadan, Catholics observe a 40 day fast during Lent. It has also been used as a gesture of political protest eg the Suffragettes, Mahatma Gandhi during the struggle for Indian independence.
